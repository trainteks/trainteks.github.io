Request Account Deletion – QuizMe

If you would like to delete your account and associated data from the QuizMe app, please follow the steps below:

  1. Send an email to support@trainteks.com from your registered account email.
  2. Use the subject line: “Account Deletion Request – QuizMe”.
  3. We will process your request within 7 business days.

What data will be deleted?

  • Your user profile (email, username)
  • Quiz history and scores
  • Settings and preferences

Retention: Once your account is deleted, all associated data is removed from our systems. We do not retain backups.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact us at support@trainteks.com.
